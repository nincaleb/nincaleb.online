How it works (quick)

Your domain uses Cloudflare Email Routing, which forwards mail to your SAGE forwarding / ingest address. You view those messages inside SAGE’s web UI.

Safety

A custom “webmail login form” on a personal site is risky and often blocked by browsers — so this page only links you to the official inbox UI.
